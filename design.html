The Starship Voyager Game

Be the captain of the starship Voyager!

In this game, you will be the captain of your own starship and you will control the flight path as you navigate through space past burning asteroids.

  • To START the game, click on "Game" at the top of the page.
  • Use the UP and DOWN arrows on the keyboard to move the starship Voyager up and down to avoid the oncoming asteroids.
  • To EXIT the game, close the browser

    • Good luck Captain!!
